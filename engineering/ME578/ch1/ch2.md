Sampling and it's Laplace representation.

Define 

$$ x^*(t) = x(t) \cdot \delta_T(t) $$

where 

$$ \delta_T(t) = \sum_{k=-\infty}^{\infty}\delta(t-kT) $$

$$ x^*(s) = \mathcal{L}\{x^*(t)\} $$
